Finished games:

Tile Wave

TileWave is a puzzle-arcade game in which you must confront an ongoing series of numbered tiles, at ever increasing speeds. You destroy tiles, and gain points, by using your in-game keyboard to form the numbers on each tile.
Download from Google Play
Download from iTunes

Space Wave

Space Wave Battles is a 2D local network space-shooter designed for the iPhone. Up to four players can play together in a classical death-match. They can choose from six unique ships in four different environments.
Download from iTunes
